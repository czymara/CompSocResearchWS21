1. Data preparation

a) Import data

Import PropPref_reduc.dta and assign it to object ESS_CMP. The main two country-level predictors of the study are political elite discourses that are exclusionary (std2_ExclusionDisc) or inclusionary (std2_InclusionDisc). All these continuous variables are standardized so that they range from 0 to 1, where 0 is the minimum of original variable and 1 the maximum of original variable. Compare ExclusionDisc (original version of variable) and std2_ExclusionDisc (standardized version of variable).

3. Interaction of political orientation and exclusionary discourse

a) Interaction model

In the paper, I argue that political elite discourses should have a stronger impact on oneâs attitude if they are congruent with oneâs existing ideology. Thus, âthe link between an exclusionary political elite discourse and attitudes should be stronger for those belonging to the political rightâ (page 1217). Add an interaction between individual ideology (std2_lrscale) and exclusionary national political discourses (std2_ExclusionDisc) to test this hypothesis. Treat both as continuous. Interpret the result.
